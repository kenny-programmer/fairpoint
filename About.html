Our History

United by a shared passion for accounting and a commitment to ethical practice, three former associates from a leading Philippine auditing firm, who were also roommates during their CPA exam preparation, founded Fairpoint. Starting with a few valued clients, the firm has steadily grown, earning their trust and building a strong reputation for providing fair, accurate, and insightful financial guidance.


Fairpoint was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide clients with fair, accurate, and insightful financial guidance. Recognizing the need for trustworthy and ethical financial partners, we established our firm on a foundation of integrity, transparency, and client-centric values.


Since our inception, we have consistently strived to exceed client expectations by delivering high-quality financial services with a focus on fairness and accuracy. We have built strong relationships with our clients by providing personalized attention, insightful advice, and a commitment to their long-term success.


Fairpoint is committed to staying at the forefront of the evolving financial landscape. We continuously invest in professional development and leverage the latest technologies to ensure our clients receive the most effective and up-to-date financial solutions.
